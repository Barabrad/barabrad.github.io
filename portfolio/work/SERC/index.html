SERC Projects

The project I worked on at SERC was the Lunar Entry and Approach Platform For Research On Ground (LEAPFROG). The main subprojects I worked on for LEAPFROG are listed in chronological order, with the oldest at the bottom:

Project Semester Group/Solo Brief Description
Live Telemetry Fall 2022 Solo We felt that there should be a better way to see telemetry than typing a series of commands.
IMU Recalibration Fall 2022 Solo If something went wrong in the initial IMU calibration, we needed a way to fix it without manually restarting the vehicle.
Delay Troubleshooting Summer 2022 Group We noticed that there was an increasing delay with each command sent.
Suicide Burn Model Summer 2022 Solo We were asked to develop an emergency landing sequence.
Sensor Coupling Summer 2022 Group The task was to determine the vehicle's actual height using data from two sensors.
Thrust Tests Spring 2022 Group We had to find out how much thrust our compressed air tanks can provide.
Pressure Measurements Spring 2022 Solo My first task was to make a separate stream in our vehicle's software for the pressure data.
